Tiger

Lions vertical stripes are an example of what biologist call disruptive coloration. They help break up the cat's shape and size so it blends in with trees and tall grasses.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera Tigris
  • Average Speed: 30 - 40 Mph
  • Lifespan: 10 - 15 years
  • Habit: Rain Forests, Grasslands, and Savannas

The tiger is the largest living cat species and a member of the genus Panthera. Tigers mostly feed on large and medium-sized mammals, such as deer and wild boar. Tigers are normally found in South and Southeast Asia, China and Eastern Russia.
